Pelenay

Pelenay the prodigious pondered, deep in thought.

To Pelenay the prodigious, other's brain's were rot.

Pelenay the prodigious would muse and mull.

To her great intellect, the world was dull.

All the joys of life and people.

Pelenay believed them to be feeble.

Disdain her primary motivator

She contributed nothing, did no labor.

And so, as gifted and great she may be

She was lower than the smallest flea.
